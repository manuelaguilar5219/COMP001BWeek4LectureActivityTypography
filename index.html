The Dodgers win the 2024 World Series!

Who did they Beat?

The Los Angeles Dodgers matched up with the New York Yankees in the World Series in 2024 for the first time since the 1981 World Series. The Los Angeles Dodgers would go on the win the best of 7 Series in 5 games.

Standout Performances

Freddie Freeman won the World Series MVP award. He hit .300 with 4 Home Runs and 12 RBI's which is tied for the most RBI's in a single World Series. In Game 1 he Shocked the world with his Game Winning Walk Off Grand Slam in the Bottom of the 10th Inning while the team was trailing 3-2.

Over all series review

Over all the Dodgers were the better team but every game was close. The Dodgers were able to capitalize on crucial Yankees Mistakes while the Yankees could not.
